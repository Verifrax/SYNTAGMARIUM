# Claim classes

This tree defines the canonical admitted claim classes for the minimum cross-stack object chain.

These files answer the constitutional-law question: what kinds of governed objects may count in the public chain, in what order, and under what admission boundaries.

## Active classes

1. law-version
2. freeze-object
3. accepted-epoch
4. authority-object
5. execution-receipt
6. verification-result
7. recognition-object
8. recourse-object
9. continuity-transfer

## Boundary

These files define admission law only.

They do not publish accepted state.
They do not issue authority objects.
They do not emit execution receipts.
They do not determine verifier outcomes.
They do not recognize or assign recourse of record.

## Use

Read `index.json` first, then inspect the individual class objects.
